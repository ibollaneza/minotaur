How It Works

Using blockchain technology, you can create a unique Minotaur linked to your digital wallet.

  1. Connect your wallet by clicking the "Connect Wallet" button.
  2. Enter a name for your Minotaur in the text box provided.
  3. Click "Generate Minotaur" to create a unique digital creature.
  4. Download or share your Minotaur with friends!
